OAN Live Cookies

Cookies are data-storage objects saved on browsers. Cookies can have a life span of just one visit or up to a time designated by the site's services. You can view and manage cookies through your browser (though some browsers for mobile devices may not offer such management visibility). We use different types of cookies to help run our OAN Live Services.

Category Details

Preferences

These cookies allow our websites to remember information that changes the way the site behaves or looks on subsequent visits. OAN Live users may notice a cookie named 'ktvcrm', 'ktvcrmi' or 'ktvcsp' that will help enhance a user's experience.

Blocking or removing these cookies may effect OAN Live's services.

Processes

Process cookies help make some of OAN Live's services work. For example, OAN Live users may see a cookie named 'ktvcrm' or 'ktvcrmi' which will help users navigate and use the site.

Blocking or removing these cookies may effect OAN Live's service experience.

Session

Session cookies often collect information about how users interact with a website. This information helps us improve our services. OAN Live users may see cookies named 'JSESSION, 'AWSELB,' and 'plumbr_user_tracker.' These cookies store unique session state identifiers and collect information on page performance and usage.

Blocking or removing these cookies may not effect OAN Live's service experience.

Analytics

OAN Live uses Google's analytics tool which helps us understand how our visitors engage with our properties and services. It may use a set of cookies to collect information and report website usage statistics without personally identifying individual visitors to Google. The main cookies used by Google Analytics is the '_ga' and '_gid' cookie.

Blocking or removing this cookie will not effect OAN Live's main services.

For more information, please visit Google's cookie information page.
