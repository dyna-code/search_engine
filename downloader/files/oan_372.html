OAN Live Privacy

OAN Live respects your preferences concerning the collection and use of personally identifiable information. This statement discloses the privacy practices for the OAN Live.com website. As part of the normal operation of this site, we may collect and, in some cases, may disclose information about you. This Privacy Policy describes the information we collect about you and how we may use or share that information. Use of OAN Live is an explicit agreement to the policy disclosed.

Registration Information

We may ask for specific Personally Identifiable Information about you when you register and create an account with OAN Live. We require that you provide user, contact and billing information, and we may ask for system information, information about your interests, preferences and future needs so that we can provide and improve our services.

Information Collected From or About You

Typically, we will collect the following information from or about you and your activities while you utilize the OAN Live Service:
  1. Information you provide, including but not limited to (name, email address, address, credit card type, and the last four digits of your card)
  2. Information automatically collected when you use the OAN Live Service (location, site activities, and other information via tracking, cookies, web beacons, or other technologies)

Newsletter Subscription Information

From time to time we provide information to our customers in the form of electronic and/or print newsletters. When you subscribe to OAN Live you will be added to our mailing list and will receive announcements and information about OAN Live. The announcements and information will come from OAN Live, not from third parties. All announcements will be sent to the email or physical address you provide while registering. You may opt out of these notices at any time by following the instruction provided at the bottom on the email notices. You can also request to stop receiving print notices or announcements by emailing support@oann.com.

Links

live.oann.com may contain links to third party sites or services. This Privacy Policy applies to the live.oann.com site only. Any information you submit to linked sites will be subject to the privacy policies of those sites, if any. OAN Live has no control over any third party site and users should review those privacy policies carefully.

Legal Disclosures

OAN Live may disclose Personally Identifiable Information if required to do so by law or in good-faith belief that such action is necessary to prevent injury or property damage. We will usually attempt to notify you when we are making such a disclosure, but we may not do so in an emergency or when we are prohibited by law or court order from giving such notice.

Passwords

Always select a password that is unlikely to be available or discovered by others, never disclose your password to any third parties. If you lose control of your password, you may lose substantial control over your Personally Identifiable Information and may be subject to legally binding actions taken or made on your behalf. No OAN Live support member will ever request your password. If you receive a request for your password from any person or company you should contact OAN Live immediately at support@oann.com. If your password has been compromised for any reason, you should immediately change your password.

Opt-Out Policy

OAN Live allows you to opt out of any marketing or promotional mailings by unsubscribing, following the links at the bottom of any email marketing or by emailing support@oann.com.

Security

OAN Live uses state-of-the-art commercially available technology to protect against unauthorized access to any Personally Identifiable Information that is stored electronically by OAN Live. OAN Live does not store any sensitive payment information. All sensitive payment information is stored by our payment processor. Regrettably, no security measures can guarantee 100% security. Accordingly, OAN Live cannot guarantee the complete security of your information and cannot assume liability for the improper or unauthorized access to it. By using the OAN Live service, or providing information to us through email, you agree that we can communicate with you electronically pertaining to matters of privacy, security, administrative, and account issues as they relate your account with OAN Live. All users are encouraged to change their password on a bi-weekly basis, to never save their username and password on a shared or public computer or device, and to delete all account information from any device before they sell it.

Children

You must be 18 years of age in order to subscribe to the OAN Live service. In certain jurisdictions the age of majority may exceed 18 years of age, in which case, you must satisfy that jurisdictions age to become a member. Individuals under the age of 18 may use the service, but they may only do so with the approval, supervision, or involvement of a parent or legal guardian, and any such use will be subject to the OAN Live Terms of Use.

Privacy Policy Changes

If we decide to change this privacy policy, we will post these changes here so that you will always know what information we gather, how we might use it and whether we will disclose it to anyone. OAN Live reserves the right to change this policy at any time without notice. Unless you opt out of receiving email notifications from us, we may also notify users by email of any significant changes to this policy.

Email

OAN Live has email links on its site to allow you to contact us directly. The information you provide is used to respond directly to your questions or comments. We may also store your comments to improve the site and our service, or review and discard the information.
